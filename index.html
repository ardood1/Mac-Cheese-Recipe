Mac & Cheese Recipe

Learn how to make a simple Mac & Cheese dish.

Ingredients

  • Kraft Mac & Cheese Dinner

Instructions

  1. Bring water to boil in medium saucepan. Add Macaroni; cook until tender, stirring occasionally.
  2. Drain. (Do not rinse.) Return macaroni to pan.
  3. Add Cheese Sauce Mix; mix well.
